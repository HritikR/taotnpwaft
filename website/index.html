The Art of Tech: Not Playing with a Full Tech

Welcome to the The Art of Tech: Not Playing with a Full Tech Facebook group website. This is a place to learn, grow, share, educate, mentor, help, etc.

The goal of this group is to have a safe place to discuss and share our tech lives, the many, many tech things we do that help us in our lives as well as others.

We are all Windows, Unix and Linux users of various levels who like to share our expertise with others no matter how basic or advanced they are
